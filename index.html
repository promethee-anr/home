UNDER CONSTRUCTION

PROMETHEE is a CNRS joint project of the Institut de Planétologie et d'Astrophysique de Grenoble (member of the Université Grenoble Alpes), the Centre de Recherche Astrophysique de Lyon and the Laboratoire d'Etudes du Rayonnement de la Matière en Astrophysique et Atmosphères. It is funded by the Agence Nationale de la Recherche for the 2023-2026 period.

Job Offers

Between 2023 and 2026 we will offer 1 three-years post-doctoral fellowship (in Paris) and 3 PhD grants funded with our ANR project. The offers will be published when available here and in the common astronomy mailing lists. Finally we will offer internships for Master students during the project period. Some of the internships may continue in PhDs.
